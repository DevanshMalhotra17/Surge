# <img src="assets/logo.png" width="50" alt="Surge Logo"> Surge: Professional Flood Safety & Command



> **Know The Water. Own The Outcome.**

Surge is an all-in-one, professional-grade flood safety and emergency response platform. Designed to provide real-time intelligence and structured safety protocols, Surge empowers survivors to prepare, respond, and recover from flood events with data-driven confidence.

---

## Core Features

### Command Center
A centralized modular suite for critical flood intelligence and property security.

*   **Surge Intel**: AI-powered location analysis combining live Weather APIs and dynamic risk assessment.
*   **HighGround**: Topographic pathfinding utilizing USGS LIDAR data to avoid low-lying dips and flood plains.
*   **The Vault**: An AES-256 encrypted digital locker for tracking emergency supplies and securing vital document backups.
*   **FlashBeacon**: A high-visibility distress signal featuring a screen stroboscope and high-frequency auditory rescue whistle.
*   **ClaimSync**: Forensic-grade damage documentation with GPS and timestamps to streamline insurance claims.

### Safety Protocols
Phase-specific guides based on FEMA, NOAA, and Red Cross guidelines:
*   **Before**: Preparation checklists, readiness self-assessments, and evacuation planning.
*   **During**: Real-time safety protocols, communication lifelines, and trapped-person response.
*   **After**: Return-home safety, damage assessment for insurance, and health hazard warnings.

### Progressive Web App & UX
*   **Offline First**: Critical safety manuals and the Vault are accessible without an active internet connection via Service Workers.
*   **Installable**: Add Surge to your home screen for instant access during emergencies.
*   **Immersive Design**: Features a custom interaction system including a kinetic cursor, scroll-driven reveal animations, and a dynamic atmospheric particle engine.
*   **Emergency SOS**: A global floating SOS bridge for immediate access to emergency protocols and hotlines.

---

## Technology Stack

*   **Logic**: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
*   **Visuals**: Modern CSS3 with high-performance animations, grain filters, and custom property systems.
*   **Architecture**: Static HTML5 with PWA integration (`manifest.json` + `sw.js`).
*   **Data Integration**: Connects with NOAA, FEMA, and USGS data nodes for real-time awareness.
