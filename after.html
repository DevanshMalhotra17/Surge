Flood Recovery Guide

Recover After The Water Recedes.

The danger doesn't end when the water goes down. Contamination, structural damage, and hidden hazards make recovery the most overlooked phase.

Wait for the all-clear — Do NOT return home until local authorities confirm it is safe. Hidden dangers persist for days.
Step 01 — Recovery Phases

Your Recovery Timeline

Recovery is a process, not a single moment. Follow these phases in order.

First 24 Hours

Official All-Clear

Stay in your shelter. Monitor local news. Do not attempt to return home until authorities explicitly confirm safety.

Day 1–3

Initial Home Assessment

Before entering, check for visible structural damage. Look for shifted foundations, cracks, or sagging.

Day 3–7

Document & Report

Photograph everything before cleaning. Contact your insurance company immediately. File claims with FEMA.
